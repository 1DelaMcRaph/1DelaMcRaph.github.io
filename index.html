African Hill Hotel & Resort is a Luxury Boutique Hotel that sits on top of Weta Hills with a panoramic view of Ho Township and Double Mountain Landscapes of Klefe environment. The Resort offer customers a privileged style lodging on the picturesque Weta Hill and serve complimentary healthy breakfast each morning. 26 impeccable rooms, each with its own African theme are ready to embrace the individual who is looking for solitude, or the adventure tourist who likes to be active. Visitors just love to drive to the Hill to view the landscape, relax, enjoy and rejuvenate the minds.
